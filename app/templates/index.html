Our Social Goals

The 5A Guarantee

The core of our product's value lies in these 5 assurances that address the major obstacles to easier, accessible therapy for the average Chinese student.

Anonymous

Students will never have to reveal their true identity to their therapists, peers or teachers at any point to get access to a therapist through our platform.

Accessible

Intuitive and as simple as a chat app, we connect students to mental health resources when they need it the most, whenever that may be.

Anytime

Students are busy during school hours, and so can't always rely on school counsellors. , and aren't reliant on their school's limited hours for this crucial resource.

Anywhere

Students don't have to be at school to access crucial mental health resources. During emergencies (or otherwise), students can access counsellors and therapists from home.

Any Platform

Through this mobile-friendly website or an iOS/Android app, students have extremely convenient & quick access to their therapists.

Our Long Term Goals

We want our platform to truly address mental health for Chinese high school students.

Widespread Coverage

We want to connect therapists in Shanghai to students in Sichuan; students from all backgrounds should get access to mental health resources.

Teach Students Self-Acceptance

Students have immense pressure on themselves to succeed. We want to remind them that no matter what happens, they can always love themselves.

Reduce Societal Pressure On Students

By showing students the importance of mental health, we want society to not place such burdens to constantly succeed.

Remove Mental Health Taboo

Many people don't reach out for fear of being judged. With our anonymous platform, we want to make asking for help normal.

Better Outcomes

We want to have a real impact by reducing the suicide rates and stress levels of students across the country.

Make Students Happier

With all the pressures students face, we want to remind them to take a moment to smell the roses from time to time.

Only The Best Therapists For Our Students.

We hold our therapists to the highest standards.


Certification

Level-2 And Level-3 Psychology Certificate Required

All our therapists have psychologist qualification certificates to practice psychology in China.

Degree

We ensure our psychologists are well trained.

We require psychological counsellors specializing in psychology have graduated with a master's degree or higher.

Experience

We ensure that psychologists on our platform have extensive experience.

We require a minimum of 400 hours of case consulting experience for psychologists on our platform.


On Job Training

Psychologists are initially supervised before independently hosting sessions.

For the first 60 hours, all sessions held are monitored and overseen by our senior psychologists.

Selection Interview

We vet and interview all psychologists before bringing them onto our platform.

All psychologists undergo a strict interview process before joining the team.
